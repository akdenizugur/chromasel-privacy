1

Information You Provide

Chromasel may ask users to enter their date of birth when the app is first opened. This information is used only to determine an appropriate age group and provide an age-appropriate advertising experience.

The full date of birth:

  • Is processed locally on the user’s device
  • Is not stored by Chromasel
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with advertising providers
  • Is not included in analytics or application logs

After the age group is calculated, only one of the following categories is stored locally on the device:

  • CHILD
  • TEEN
  • ADULT
  • UNKNOWN

This age category is stored locally so that the app does not need to request the date of birth every time it is opened. It may remain on the device until the app is uninstalled, its storage is cleared, or the application data is reset.

2

Age-Appropriate Experience

Chromasel is intended for a mixed audience that may include children, teenagers, and adults.

The app uses a neutral age screen before initializing advertising services. Advertising services are not initialized and advertisements are not requested until the user’s age category has been determined.

For children and users whose age cannot be determined, Chromasel configures advertising requests as child-directed and applies additional restrictions designed to prevent personalized or interest-based advertising.

Teen users are also treated as being under the applicable age of consent for advertising purposes.

All age groups are restricted to advertisements with a maximum content rating of “G – General Audiences.”
3

Advertising and Google AdMob

Chromasel uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements.

Depending on the user’s age category, consent choices, device settings, location, and applicable law, Google AdMob may process information such as:

  • IP address and approximate location derived from the IP address
  • Device or application identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers, where permitted
  • Application interactions, including app launches, taps, and ad views
  • Diagnostic and performance information
  • Information used for advertising, analytics, security, and fraud prevention

Chromasel configures Google AdMob to limit personalized advertising and identifier use for children and users of unknown age.

Advertisements shown to children and users of unknown age are configured to use child-appropriate advertising settings and a “G – General Audiences” content rating.

Google processes information according to its own privacy policies, advertising policies, and terms.

4

Advertising Consent and Privacy Choices

Chromasel uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (“UMP”) to manage advertising privacy choices where required.

Adult users in certain countries or regions may be shown a consent message before advertising services are initialized. This message may allow users to:

  • Consent to personalized advertising
  • Refuse consent
  • Manage advertising and data-processing options
  • Review the advertising technology providers involved

Where required, users may later reopen the privacy form through the “Ad Privacy Preferences” or “Reklam Gizlilik Tercihleri” option in the application settings.

If consent is required and permission to request advertisements has not been provided, Chromasel does not initialize the advertising service or request advertisements.

Children, teenagers, and users of unknown age are not shown an adult advertising consent flow. They are instead placed into the restricted, age-appropriate advertising configuration.

5

Images and Artwork

Chromasel allows users to select images from their device and save completed artwork to their device.

Images selected by the user and artwork created in Chromasel are processed locally on the device.

Chromasel does not upload selected images or completed artwork to our servers.

When a user chooses to save an image, the app may request permission to add the completed artwork to the device’s photo or media library.

If the user chooses to share an image using another application or service, the handling of that image is governed by the privacy policy of the selected third-party application or service.

6

Device Permissions

Chromasel may request limited device permissions that are necessary for features such as:

  • Selecting an image
  • Saving completed artwork
  • Displaying advertisements
  • Accessing network connectivity

Chromasel does not require continuous access to the user’s entire photo or video library. Media access is intended only for user-initiated image selection and saving operations.

Users can review or revoke application permissions through their device settings. Revoking a permission may prevent the related feature from functioning.

7

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Chromasel itself does not operate website cookies inside the application.

Third-party advertising and consent services may use device storage, identifiers, SDK storage, or similar technologies to:

  • Store consent choices
  • Deliver and measure advertisements
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Maintain advertising and privacy preferences

The availability and use of these technologies may depend on the user’s age category, consent choices, device settings, and applicable law.

8

Children’s Privacy

Chromasel does not intentionally request names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location information, or account credentials from children through the age-screening process.

The full date of birth entered on the neutral age screen is not retained or transmitted.

For users identified as children or whose age is unknown:

  • Personalized and interest-based advertising is restricted
  • Child-directed advertising settings are applied
  • The maximum advertisement content rating is set to G
  • Advertising services are not initialized before the age category is determined
  • Advertising identifiers are restricted in accordance with the applied SDK settings

Parents or legal guardians who believe that a child has provided personal information through Chromasel may contact us using the email address below.

9

Data Sharing

Chromasel does not operate a system that directly sells user-submitted personal information.

Information may be processed by Google AdMob and Google UMP as necessary to provide advertisements, manage privacy choices, perform analytics, maintain security, and prevent fraud.

Third-party providers process information under their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

10

Data Retention

The derived age category is stored locally on the user’s device and may remain until:

  • The application is uninstalled
  • The application’s storage is cleared
  • The age-category data is reset during development or troubleshooting

Advertising consent choices may be retained by Google UMP or related Google services according to their own retention practices.

Chromasel does not retain the user’s complete date of birth on its servers.

11

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by Chromasel.

However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users should also protect their devices and keep their operating systems and applications updated.

User Choices

  • Reopen advertising privacy preferences through the application settings
  • Disable or reset the device advertising identifier through Android settings
  • Revoke application permissions through device settings
  • Clear locally stored application data
  • Uninstall Chromasel

Some application or advertising features may become unavailable after certain permissions or consent choices are changed.
